Privacy Policy

At Memo, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and other identifiable details you provide.
  • Non-personal Information: IP address, browser type, location data, and usage statistics.
  • Cookies: Small text files to enhance your experience and analyze usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for:

  • Improving our services and user experience.
  • Providing personalized content or recommendations.
  • Sending updates, newsletters, or promotional messages (only with your consent).
  • Monitoring website performance and analytics.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • To trusted service providers who assist in running our platform.
  • To comply with legal requirements or law enforcement requests.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect your information. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request data deletion or opt-out of communications.
  • Adjust cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Cookies

Cookies help us optimize your experience on our website. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or disable cookies entirely.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with the updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at info@memo.com.
